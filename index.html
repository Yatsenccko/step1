  • Web design is the process of creating the visual part of a website, including developing a layout, selecting colors, typography, and graphics to make the website aesthetically pleasing and easy to use for visitors. Web design involves both technical and artistic skills, such as understanding user experience, usability principles, and how to use various design software and programming languages.
  • Graphic design is the art and practice of creating visual content to communicate information and ideas to a specific audience. Graphic designers use typography, images, colors, and other design elements to create designs for a variety of media, such as advertising, websites, packaging, logos, and more.
  • Online support refers to the assistance provided to customers or users via digital channels, such as websites, chatbots, email, social media, or other online communication tools. Online support is a key part of customer service for businesses that operate online, as it provides a quick and convenient way for customers to get help and resolve their issues.
  • App design refers to the process of creating the visual and functional elements of a mobile application. App design involves several stages, including conceptualization, wireframing, prototyping, and development. The design of a mobile app is critical to its success, as it determines how users interact with the app and how it meets their needs.
  • Online marketing, also known as digital marketing, refers to the practice of promoting products, services, or brands through digital channels, such as search engines, social media, email, and websites. Online marketing involves a range of techniques and strategies that help businesses reach their target audience, build brand awareness, and drive sales.
  • SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, refers to the practice of improving the visibility and ranking of a website or web page on search engine results pages (SERPs) such as Google, Bing, or Yahoo. SEO services are typically offered by digital marketing agencies or SEO specialists who are experts in optimizing websites to improve their visibility on search engines.
